# Senior Software Engineer — Developer Platform

We are hiring a Senior Software Engineer to join our Developer Platform team. You will design and maintain the SDKs, CLIs, and developer tools that thousands of internal and external engineers rely on every day.

## Responsibilities
- Design and ship developer-facing SDKs in Python and TypeScript with rigorous API ergonomics.
- Build and operate the CLI tooling, package management workflows, and code-completion services that power our developer ecosystem.
- Own the technical roadmap for the platform: prioritize work that reduces friction for app developers building on top of our APIs.
- Partner closely with firmware, backend, and product teams to define APIs that are intuitive, well-documented, and forwards-compatible.
- Drive engineering standards through code review, technical design reviews, and clear documentation.
- Mentor junior and mid-level engineers on developer-experience best practices.

## Requirements (must have)
- 7+ years of professional software engineering experience.
- Strong proficiency in **Python** and **TypeScript**.
- Experience designing and shipping developer SDKs or API clients used by external engineers.
- Track record building **CLI tools, build systems, or package management** workflows.
- Experience with **REST API design** and developer ecosystems at scale.
- Demonstrated ability to lead cross-team technical initiatives.

## Nice to have
- Experience with browser-based IDEs or web-based developer tooling.
- Familiarity with Go for systems-level work.
- Background in firmware-adjacent platforms (embedded, IoT, wearables).
- Experience with CI/CD infrastructure and automated testing tooling.

## What you'll bring
- A strong sense of ownership and a bias toward high-leverage tooling work.
- Excellent written technical communication.
- Passion for developer experience and reducing friction at scale.
